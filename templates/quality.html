Quality: About This Data Set

In this byte, I explore and clean the dataset mainly by Wrangler and python. Speaking of the quality of the data set, we mainly focus on three aspects: completeness, coherency and correctness.

Completeness. The data contains many features originally (and even after cleaned). But many of the columns contain missing values.

Coherency. The data is mostly coherent, though it has some redundancy, it's very reasonable and the features of it are highly relative to each other.

Correctness. Due to the accountability of the data source, the data is correct. And the analysis can also validate its correctness.

Is the data complete?

No. The data isn't complete. The columns containing missing data includes: Latitude, Longitude, Aircraft damage, Engine Type, FAR Description, Purpose of Flight, Air Carrier, Total Fatal Injuries, Total Serious Injuries, Total Minor Injuries, Total Uninjured, Weather Condition, Broad Phase of Flight, Airport Name, Airport Code, Aircraft Damage, Number of engines.

#98 lacks state and city information. I guess because the location is Atlantic Oceans, so the city column is left blank.

And the Amateur built column only records No and is blank when the answer is yes.

I think the incomepleteness of data may due to personal factors. And it may also because the google fusion table lost some parts of data when importing dataset from NTSB.

Is the data coherent?

Yes, the data makes sense relative to itself. For example, the location, airport name and country always match each other. And the Injury Severity matches with Total Fatal Injuries, Total Serious Injuries, Total Minor Injuries, Total Uninjured. As shown in the chart below.

This result also matches my expectations. The distribution is sensible because the fatal events occur least, as shown below.

Is the data correct?

Consider the dataset is maintained by a highly-trustable institue, the complete part of the data is supposed to be correct. I randomly pick 10 event ids from this data set and validate them with the information on the Internet. The result is that all the features match with the information online and the data provided by other source. Random sample can really tell something.

While searching on the Internet, I find out that some data from other souce is complete. This may prove that the incompleteness of this dataset may due to bad recording or loss of data while importing and exporting.
